The Core Idea

Sustainable behaviour has two problems.

It's invisible — you can't see that skipping a car ride saved CO₂. And it feels sacrificial — there's no immediate reward for doing it.

GreenWallet solves both. It makes your environmental impact visible in real numbers, and it rewards you with points and money saved so the behaviour feels worth it. Think of it as a loyalty programme — but for the planet.

The Four Categories

What gets tracked, and why it matters.

Every category uses India-specific data — because your emissions reality is different from Europe or the US, and your app should reflect that.

🚌
Transport

The biggest impact category. Every time you choose public transport, cycling, or walking over a private vehicle, GreenWallet calculates the CO₂ you kept out of the atmosphere and the money you didn't spend on fuel or a cab.

Highest points per action
⚡
Electricity

India's grid is heavily coal-dependent, which means every unit of electricity you save has a bigger climate impact here than almost anywhere else. Log units saved and see the real CO₂ reduction — calculated against India's actual grid data.

India grid factor applied
💧
Water Conservation

Water and energy are inseparable — treating, pumping, and heating water all consume electricity. In a water-stressed country like India, conservation has both an environmental and a social dimension. Log your actions, see your impact.

Water-energy impact tracked
☀️
Solar Generation

Generating clean energy is more impactful than just saving dirty energy. If you have a solar panel, log your daily generation and see exactly how much coal power you displaced from India's grid.

Premium points · highest impact
The Points System

Points that mean something.

Green Points aren't random — they reflect real environmental impact. Actions with higher CO₂ savings, higher money saved, and greater behavioural effort earn more. The system is weighted to reward what actually matters.

🚌
Transport earns the most

Choosing public transport over a car has the highest combined CO₂ and money impact — so it earns the highest points. The longer the distance, the greater the reward.

☀️
Solar is the premium category

Generating clean energy is more valuable than just saving it. Solar actions carry a premium multiplier because you're actively adding clean power to the equation.

⚡
Electricity saves add up fast

Because India's grid is coal-heavy, saving electricity here matters more than in most countries. Consistent daily savings accumulate into significant CO₂ reductions.

💧
Water builds the habit

Smaller points per action, but water conservation builds the daily logging habit that makes the whole system work. Every litre tracked is a litre of embedded energy saved.

How It Works

Log once. See everything update live.

From the moment you tap submit to the moment your wallet reflects your impact — it all happens in seconds, automatically.

1
Pick a category and log your action
Select transport, electricity, water, or solar. Enter what you did — a trip, units saved, litres conserved. It takes about 10 seconds.
2
Your impact is calculated instantly
The app runs the numbers immediately — CO₂ saved, money saved, and points earned — using India-specific data built into the platform.
3
Your wallet updates in real time
Your Green Points, total CO₂ saved, and money saved all update the moment you log. No refresh needed. Your history is always current.
4
Badges unlock automatically
Hit a milestone — first action, a CO₂ threshold, a points total — and a badge appears in your profile without you having to do anything. The app tracks it all.
5
Come back and keep going
Your data is saved to your account. Every session adds to your lifetime impact. The longer you use it, the more meaningful the numbers become.
